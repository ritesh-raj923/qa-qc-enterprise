    📋 QA/QC Inspection Suite

    Complete Quality Management System for Construction Projects

    🏗️ About This Application

    The QA/QC Inspection Suite is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline quality management processes in construction projects. It replaces manual, paper-based systems with automated workflows, real-time collaboration, and secure data management.

    Built as a Progressive Web App (PWA), it works on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones – enabling field engineers to log data instantly from any location.

    ⚡ Key Features

    📄
    RFI Management
    Submit, review & approve inspections
    📋
    NCR Tracking
    Identify & close non-conformances
    📊
    Project Audits
    Comprehensive site audits with 56+ checkpoints
    ✅
    Material Approval
    IMIR submissions & client approval
    📎
    Activity Checklists
    Painting, Tiling, Door Frames & more
    🔔
    Real-Time Notifications
    Instant alerts for approvals & assignments

    🔄 Workflow Overview

    📄 RFI Flow

    Draft → Submitted → Approved by Execution → Approved/Rejected

    Contractor submits → Execution Engineer reviews → QA Head approves.

    📋 NCR Flow

    Draft → Open → Under Review → Closed/Rejected

    QA raises → Contractor responds → QA approves or rejects.

    📊 Audit Flow

    Draft → Open → Under Review → Closed/Rejected

    Manager/QA raises → Agency responds → Manager/QA approves or rejects.

    ✅ IMIR Flow

    Draft → Submitted → Approved/Rejected

    Material submitted for client approval.

    🚀 How To Get Started

    1. Sign Up – Create an account with your email, role, and assigned sites.
    2. Wait for Approval – An admin will approve your account.
    3. Login – Access your dashboard and start creating reports.
    4. Submit RFI/NCR/Audit – Follow the automated workflow.
    5. Review & Approve – Based on your role, approve or reject submissions.
    6. Track & Report – Use the Saved Records page to search, filter, and print reports.
